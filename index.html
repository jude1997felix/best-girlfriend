Happy Women's Day ❤️

Happy Women’s Day, my beautiful ponnu,
In every little moment, I’m thankful for you.

Your smile shines bright like the warm morning sun,
En nenjil nee dhaan always number one.

When you laugh, the whole world feels light and new,
Un kural kettaale my heart feels true.

Life may rush fast, or sometimes move slow,
Aana un kooda irundhaale my happiness will grow.

So today I’ll say what my heart always knew,
En life-la the best blessing… is simply you. ❤️
